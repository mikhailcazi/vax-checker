VaxChecker

A tool to periodically check availability of COVID-19 vaccination slots in India.
Enter your district ID and click 'Start Search'.
If you do not know your district ID, click the 'Get District ID List' button on the right to get a list of districts and corresponding IDs.
The application will send desktop notifications (if enabled) whenever an available slot has been encountered.
